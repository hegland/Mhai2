### Attendance & Online Participation

This group is designed to be an **in-person experience** to maximize the benefits of community and shared learning. Online attendance via Zoom is available **only** under the following circumstances:

- You are unwell (to protect the health of the group)
- You are interstate for work purposes

**Notification of non-attendance is required.** If you are unable to attend in person or need to join online for the reasons above, please notify the team as soon as possible.

### Online Guidelines & Safety

Should you need to join virtually, the following guidelines are essential for the safety and confidentiality of all members:

- **Privacy:** Please ensure you are in a private, quiet space where you will not be interrupted. No other person should be able to see or hear the group proceedings.

- **Headphones:** If other people are in your home/location, you must use headphones to ensure that private group content is not overheard.

- **Camera Usage:** Your camera must remain on, and you must be visible for the duration of the session. This is a safety requirement.

- **Comfort Items:** You are welcome to have comfort items (pets, fidget toys, tea) to help you stay grounded, provided they do not distract you from active participation.

### Materials

You will be provided with a comprehensive skills manual containing the full 22-week curriculum. You are required to bring this book with you to every session, as we will be working directly from it each week.
